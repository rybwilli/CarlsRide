About Carl

Carl was a beloved husband, father, friend, and cycling enthusiast who could turn any ordinary Saturday into an adventure on two wheels. Whether it was a trail ride trough the trees, a mellow city beer ride, or an epic road challenge, he showed up with a grin and made everyone feel like they belonged. Carl passed away in a tragic accident Oct 31 2025 and we all miss him dearly.

He believed riding bikes was one of the best ways to connect with people — not because of the miles or the pace, but because of the conversations along the way and the camaraderie at the end.

These rides are a celebration of Carl — his spirit, his love of community, and his knack for getting out on bikes. We ride in his honor, and we eat well afterwards, just the way he would have wanted. Carl is survived by his wife, Lisa, and his daughter, Chloe (pictured)
